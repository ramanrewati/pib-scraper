President’s Greetings on the Eve of New Year

Posted On: 31 DEC 2019 6:48PM by PIB Delhi

The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind has greeted fellow citizens on the eve of New Year 2020. 

In a message the President said, “On the eve of the New Year, I extend my greetings and best wishes to all fellow Indians and the global community. 

The dawn of New Year-and the new decade is an occasion to commit ourselves to the making of a society that is peaceful, caring and compassionate. This is also an apt occasion to renew our commitment towards a stronger and more prosperous India. 

May the New Year bring joy and prosperity in your lives! ”.
